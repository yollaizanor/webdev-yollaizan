Overview of the museum

Welcome to the Heritage Town Museum where Sydney’s vibrant history and cultural legacy come to life. Established in 1999, our museum was founded with a vision to preserve and celebrate the stories that shaped this city—from its ancient Indigenous roots and colonial past to its modern evolution as a global metropolis.

Nestled in the heart of Sydney, our museum serves as a gateway to the city’s diverse heritage. Over the years, we have grown from a small archival space into a dynamic institution offering world-class exhibitions, interactive displays, and community events. With a collection that spans centuries, we aim to inspire curiosity, spark conversation, and deepen understanding of the rich tapestry of cultures and histories that define Sydney.

Our Mission

Honor the Sydney’s past, engage with its present, and inspire its future by preserving and showcasing the city’s historical and cultural treasures. Through immersive exhibits, educational programs, and community collaborations, we amplify the diverse voices and stories that shape Sydney, creating spaces for learning, dialogue, and discovery for visitors of all ages.
